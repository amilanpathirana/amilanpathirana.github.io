How to entertain a two year old?

Amila Pathirana

Aug 9, 2020 • 4 min read

Toddlers mainly interact with their parents. As of second birthday they start to walk and with no time they are running all over the house. Since they started exploring the world at greater pace, they are subjected to more information. If they can't speak at that time, they will make sounds and seek parents attention. If they can speak, they are always calling mom, dad or siblings to interact with. This is the best time to introduce him to Â fun learning activities. The parents and grand parents are the best teachers for the child of this age. They should not only spend time with them, they should give their total attention to them for certain periods of time. This extra attention will show observable Â increase of the mental development of the two year old's.

What are the best activities to do with two year old's?

  1. Cook with your two year old.

When you are working in the kitchen, give them a cooking pan to make their own meals. Â Cooking pan half full of water, onion skins, potato skins, carrot, potato and Â a big spoon will do the trick. Change this recipe according to what you are cooking in the kitchen. Â There is no difference between girls and boys. Any two year old will enjoy this activity. They will get little bit wet. They will make little bit of mess. But it's a good learning activity for them. As they stir the cauldron, you can teach them the types of vegetables. This is my favorite way of keeping my kid entertained while I work in the kitchen.

2. Go on a walk with him/her and talk about the surroundings.

Go for a short walks with him/her. Â you can use toy vehicle to put him on and push along with you. If the two-three year old can walk, let him walk with you. If you are walking with them, they may get tired after a while and remember to carry him for a while until he/she is ready to walk again. While walking, ask your two year old what he is seeing and let he/she know what is correct and incorrect. For example- you can show them birds, crows, planes, big trucks, yellow bulldozers, black pickup trucks and so on.

3. Build a playhouse with your two year old and play with them

This may not be a very expensive project. Big cardboard box in the basement will do the trick. If you and your two year old likes outdoors, you can build a simple play house from cheap wood. If you have no experience with woodwork, this may be a very good experience for you to lean as well. Following is the one I build for my two year old. I had no prior experience doing such work, but at the end both you and your kid learned some new things. During the construction phase let him observe what you are doing. He/she may be so eager to join you and help you, but get a help from your spouse and keep him away from any danger. But when you finished using the hacksaw, let he/she come close to you. Let he/she to pretend like they are helping.

4. Give your two year old a board to scribble.

Give your two year old a board to scribble. You can draw with the child as well. When I do this, I draw simple shapes like circles and call it a circle. Â He/she can draw whatever he/she wants. This is a good way to get him familiarize with the Alphabet and numbers. Remember not to force him/her to learn. This should be a just a fun activity for them. After a while your child will ask you to draw a circle, letter B, number 5, etc. That means he knows those shapes which is good thing.

5. Ask your two year old to do small things by himself/herself.

For example- Bringing a book/cup/toy to mom or dad. From this he/she will learn to follow simple instructions. When they complete the task successfully, give them a pat on the head and say "Good job" or something along the way. Remember this is not training your two year old to do your chores. It is an exercise to team him language and discipline.

6. Playing in the garden with your two year old.

Play in the garden with your two year old. Â On the sand with toys or in the water with toys. No expensive or new toys. Â Catch and throw balls, run around , chase him and let him chase you. If you have a wheelbarrow hang around in your backyard, that wheelbarrow is worth more than 500$ flimsy electric toy car. This is one of the best ways to keep your two year old entertained for a while.
