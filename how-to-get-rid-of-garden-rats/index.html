How to get rid of garden rats naturally?

Amila Pathirana

Apr 17, 2021 • 2 min read

I had quite a bit of trouble with garden rats.  After trying so many natural options, the one thing that worked was peppermint Oil. Just pour a  bottle of peppermint oil  into a couple of cups of water and put it in a spray bottle. Then spray around the affected area. My garden has small garden area where rats make borrows during the winter. After spraying a bottle of peppermint essential oils around the area. there are no more rats. Rats seems to be really repulsed by the peppermint smell. Apart from this I did found few other ways to get rid of these pesky rats.

Getting a CAT to get rid of the garden rats

Getting a cat seems to be a obvious  answer to the question, but while the cats have the ratting & moussing instinct, most of the cats have no idea how to hunt rats. If you really want to use a cat, getting a cat that owned by a farmer may do the trick. Anyhow, do not get any cat. Get a cat that trained to catch rats.

Get rid of  whatever attracting these rats

Rats are in your yard probably because the is junk or garden waste lying around in the corners of your yard. Getting rid of these rat shelters should be your number one priority before using any other methods. If you are really lucky, This is the only step you need to leave your yard.

Using a glass container to get rid of rats

another way i have tried with bit of success  to trap them with a deep glass container. You need to put something tasty  inside the glass container to attract the rats into it . Make sure you check the trap at least once a day. A trapped mouse will kill itself trying to escape a confined space and you wont like the smell of dead rats.

Humane rat traps to catch rats

If you have a pest store near your area, check for humane rat traps. These humane rat traps are intended to catch and release the rats and mouse. The downside is you have to find a area to release them. Its not a good idea to release them back in to your neighbors yard.  Best thing is to drive to a forest area and release them. This method is bit inconvenient, but really effective especially if you don't want to kill the rats.
