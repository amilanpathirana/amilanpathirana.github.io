Safest Of Them All

Amila Pathirana

Apr 24, 2020 • 1 min read

Remember this wind turbine fire that killed two? No right? How about famous Fukushima disaster? Everyone knows about it.

This wind turbine accident alone killed two people , compared to Fukushima's zero. Nuclear power is ridiculously safe. One of the worst disasters happen to a nuclear power plant and didn't killed anybody from it.

Some people would say "What’s about terrorists and planes?" What are they going to with the plane? A plane won’t scratch the containment. Two meter thick reinforced concrete walls can withstand just about anything, and that’s not even the reactor yet; the reactor itself is made of thick steel.

Some would say “But it’s so much more expensive?”  I thought we wanted to save the environment, not save money. Money does not seems to be a problem when they are used to build expensive solar and wind farms.

Some would say “But what about waste?” As it stands, that waste isn’t going anywhere - which is good. We can contain it, regulate it, and monitor it until we can store it.
